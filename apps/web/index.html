Free Online Cubing Tools

Cubing World provides free, professional-grade tools for Rubik's Cube speedcubers of all levels. Whether you're just starting out or training for competition, our tools help you practice smarter.

Scramble Generator

Generate random, WCA-compliant scrambles for your 3x3 Rubik's Cube. Our scramble generator features:

  • 20-move random state scrambles
  • Interactive 3D cube visualization
  • Step-by-step scramble playback
  • Adjustable animation speed

Speedcubing Timer

Precision timer for tracking your solve times. Features include:

  • Keyboard activation (hold Space to start)
  • Automatic scramble generation
  • Session statistics (mo3, ao5, ao12)
  • Personal best tracking
  • Solve history with scramble records

Speedcubing Timer - Track Your Solves

Precision timer for tracking your Rubik's Cube solve times, just like in WCA competitions. Improve your speedcubing skills by analyzing your performance with detailed statistics.

Features:

  • Keyboard activation - hold Space to start, release to begin solving
  • Automatic scramble generation after each solve
  • Session statistics: mo3 (mean of 3), ao5 (average of 5), ao12 (average of 12)
  • Personal best (PB) tracking for single solves and averages
  • Complete solve history with scramble records for each attempt
  • Delete times or mark as DNF (Did Not Finish) or +2 penalty
